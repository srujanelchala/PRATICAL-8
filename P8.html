Vision

To be a leading centre of learning that empowers students to become innovative, ethical and socially responsible professionals who contribute to national and global development.

Mission

Quality Education

Provide industry-relevant curriculum, research opportunities and excellent teaching-learning practices.

Holistic Development

Encourage co-curricular activities and life-skills that shape well- rounded individuals.

Community Engagement

Foster partnerships with industry and community to solve real- world problems.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • PSO1: Apply core disciplinary knowledge to design and develop solutions for engineering problems.
  • PSO2: Demonstrate professional skills, teamwork, and ethical practices in multidisciplinary environments.
  • PSO3: Engage in lifelong learning and research to adapt to technological advancements.
